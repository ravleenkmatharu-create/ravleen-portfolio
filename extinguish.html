Health Tech Mobile App Research

Extinguish

A firefighter-focused app for logging environmental exposures and supporting long-term health monitoring.

Timeline Fall 2023 · 6 Weeks
Role UX Researcher & Designer
Platform iOS Mobile App
Tools Figma, Miro
🔥
🩺

Overview

The Problem

Firefighters face a silent epidemic — chronic exposure to carcinogens, toxic smoke, and hazardous chemicals that accumulate over a career. Most lack a reliable way to log these exposures or track patterns over time, making it difficult to advocate for their own health with medical professionals.

The Goal

Design a mobile app that empowers firefighters to log environmental exposures after incidents, visualize their history, and generate sharable health reports — helping them take proactive control of their long-term wellbeing.

Discovery

Understanding the User

I began with contextual research — reading about firefighter occupational health, reviewing studies on cancer rates in the profession, and conducting interviews to understand the daily reality of exposure tracking.

Design Process

From Insight to Interface

With research findings in hand, I mapped user flows, sketched low-fidelity concepts, and iterated through wireframes before moving into high-fidelity prototypes in Figma.

01

Affinity Mapping

Synthesized interview data to identify recurring themes around pain points, behaviors, and motivations.

02

User Flow Mapping

Defined the core task flows: incident logging, exposure history review, and report generation.

03

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity sketches focusing on speed of entry — users needed to log an exposure in under 60 seconds.

04

Prototyping & Testing

Built interactive Figma prototypes and conducted usability tests with 4 participants to validate and refine.

Solution

Key Design Decisions

🔥

Quick-Log Incident Entry

A streamlined form reduces entry to essential fields — incident type, substances, duration, and protective gear worn. Designed to be completed in under 60 seconds post-incident.

📊

Exposure Timeline

A visual history of logged exposures, grouped by incident type and date. Firefighters can spot patterns and share their timeline directly with healthcare providers.

📄

Shareable Health Reports

One-tap PDF report generation summarizing exposure history in medical-friendly language — bridging the gap between field experience and clinical conversation.

Reflection

What I Learned

This project reinforced how constraints can sharpen design. The 6-week timeline forced prioritization — I had to identify the single most valuable feature set and execute it well rather than building everything. Designing for a high-stress user context also deepened my appreciation for cognitive load reduction: every screen needed to work for someone who is tired, stressed, and pressed for time.
