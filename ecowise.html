Energy Insight — EcoWise

An advanced energy monitoring platform designed to help manufacturing teams enhance energy efficiency, reduce operational downtime, and accelerate data-driven decision-making. It delivers a unified and intuitive solution for comprehensive operational insight.

Role UI/UX Researcher & Designer
Timeline 3 Months — Spring 2024
Team 3 design students + Energy Insight team
Platform Web Dashboard
Tools Figma · Miro · Notion

Project Overview

Energy Insights is an Indiana-based energy technology company that provides IoT-powered solutions for small to mid-sized manufacturers. While their original starter kit helped reduce energy costs, the platform faced key challenges in usability, long-term support, and business scalability, especially after grant funding ended.

The team reimagined the solution as EcoWise, an intelligent, AI-enhanced platform featuring real-time insights and a built-in generative AI assistant. This redesigned experience enables manufacturers to monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize their energy usage independently, with minimal training or outside support.

With a focus on scalability and ease of use, EcoWise centralizes energy visibility, troubleshooting, and actionable intelligence within a streamlined interface.

Key Insight

Its intelligent alert system and integrated AI assistant enable users at all technical levels to manage energy performance confidently, eliminating dependence on external consultants or extensive training.

Business Model

EcoWise is positioned as a scalable B2B SaaS platform designed for industrial clients operating in smart manufacturing environments. Built to support long-term client partnerships, the model is focused on recurring revenue, energy optimization, and operational efficiency.

01

Subscription-Based Tiers. Flexible pricing based on factory size, usage level, and feature needs. Entry-level plans include essential monitoring tools, while advanced tiers include AI diagnostics and AI Nova chatbot support.

02

AI-as-a-Service. Premium tools powered by AI Nova unlock predictive maintenance, root-cause analysis, and proactive alert systems to reduce downtime.

03

Integration Services. Premium onboarding and technical deployment ensure success with custom setup, implementation support, and seamless integration with IoT systems and Manufacturing Execution Systems platforms.

04

Data Analytics Add-Ons. Advanced analytics packages for large-scale manufacturers include benchmarking against industry standards, automated compliance reporting, and optimization consulting.

Research Findings

To better understand the real-world challenges users face with energy monitoring tools, interviews were conducted with key stakeholders, system integrators, and hardware/software providers. The goal was to identify pain points, unmet needs, and expectations across different roles involved in energy management—from daily operators to decision-makers.

Secondary Research analyzed industry trends, market demands, economic forces, and the competitive landscape shaping the smart energy sector. Key findings revealed the growing demand for IoT-based energy solutions driven by the need for energy efficiency, compliance, and data security in user-friendly, adaptable systems.

Primary Research involved in-depth interviews examining how users interact with current energy systems, identifying pain points around data access and usability, and uncovering expectations for future improvements. The growing preference for eco-friendly practices and increased government incentives for energy efficiency are reshaping demands for energy optimization and conservation.

Key Finding

The growing preference for eco-friendly practices and increased government incentives for energy efficiency are reshaping demands for energy optimization and conservation — directly validating the market opportunity for EcoWise.

Ideation & Concept Development

With core problem areas identified, the team entered the ideation phase, blending problem framing and brainstorming in a collaborative, hands-on sprint. The Crazy 8's method was used to quickly explore a wide range of solutions, from AI-powered chat assistants to adaptive dashboards that respond to user roles and proximity.

Through stakeholder interviews and peer critiques, recurring themes emerged that would shape our design direction.

Role-Specific Dashboards Operators prefer simple, role-specific views tailored to their team's needs
Customization & Control Engineers and management need flexibility and control over the system
Learning Curve New tools take time to learn — information overload impacts decisions
Pricing Transparency Grant end brings unexpected charges, often leading to system abandonment

"How might we support manufacturing teams in understanding energy usage and maintaining efficiency after the grant period, without relying on constant external support or complex dashboards?"

Key Insight

Through a combination of stakeholder interviews and secondary research, core problem areas and opportunities were identified.

Different users need role-specific interfaces tailored to their skill level and responsibilities. The system must scale beyond the pilot and reduce reliance on consultants.

Core Principle

The solution must deliver long-term value, not just display static data. Support and insights should be automated, contextual, and continuous — enabling independent operation without external consultants.

Solution

The EcoWise Dashboard offers a suite of powerful features that empower manufacturers to achieve lasting improvements in energy management and efficiency. Each feature is designed to reduce manual burden and enable confident, independent decision-making.

01

Streamlined Onboarding & First-Time Setup

New users are welcomed with a guided onboarding flow designed to minimize setup time and build confidence from the start. Walks users through selecting their role and energy priorities, auto-configures dashboard views and alert settings based on preferences, and provides a step-by-step tour of key features, including AI Nova and machine-level insights.

02

Dashboard with Machine-Level Focus

The dashboard provides a live snapshot of energy usage across the manufacturing floor, enabling users to monitor, compare, and act on machine performance. Displays energy status and alerts for each machine in a simplified, visual layout. Let users click into individual machines for detailed metrics and historical trends. Offers filter and sorting options to prioritize what needs attention most.

03 Signature Feature

AI Nova Assistant

AI Nova is a built-in, conversational assistant that provides real-time support, helping users make sense of their energy data and resolve issues more quickly. True to its name, Nova's mission is to bring clarity at critical moments, illuminating complex insights and guiding users toward confident, informed action. Offers step-by-step troubleshooting, learns from plant-level behavior, and acts as a knowledge hub reducing reliance on external support.

04

Smart Alert System: Real-Time Voltage Warnings

Our IoT-powered system continuously monitors electrical health to detect anomalies and prevent issues before they escalate. Detects abnormal behavior like consistently elevated voltage levels. Displays critical alerts with clear context—machine name, issue severity, and easy-to-understand explanations. Directs users to the integrated Help Center or AI Nova Assistant for instant support and guidance.
