Hi, I'm Ravleen.

I'm a UX designer who focuses on clarity, structure, and making complex things feel simple. I research, listen to users, and dig into real problems — then design solutions that actually work.

I'm drawn to problems that have layers — multiple users, competing needs, or systems where structure directly changes how people make decisions.

I've designed across different domains — a health-tech app for firefighters, an AI-enhanced energy platform, a gesture-driven access control tool. Different problems, one consistent approach: understand what's actually needed, design it well, make sure it holds up.

User Research · Interaction Design · Prototyping · Figma · Usability Testing · Design Systems
