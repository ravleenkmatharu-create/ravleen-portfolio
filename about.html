Hi, I am Ravleen.

I am a UX designer focused on clarity, structure, and making complex things feel simple. I like working on problems that aren't straightforward, where there are multiple users, different needs, and decisions depend on how clearly things are presented. That's where design really starts to matter.

My process usually starts with understanding what's actually going on. I talk to users, dig into the problem, and figure out what's missing. From there, I focus on shaping solutions that are clear, usable, and hold up in real scenarios.

I have worked across different domains, from a health-tech app for firefighters to an AI-driven energy platform and a gesture-based access system. Different contexts, but the same approach: understand the problem, structure it well, and design something that makes sense.

User Research · Interaction Design · Prototyping · Figma · Usability Testing · Design Systems

How I Think

Most design problems aren't really UI problems. They are structural ones. Unclear flows, competing priorities, and missing connections between parts.

I usually start by mapping the system before focusing on individual screens. Once the structure is clear, simplicity becomes obvious. I am not trying to make things look minimal, I am trying to make them make sense.
